What is SolisImperium?

SolisImperium was an SMP server that wasn't really the best. When I joined, I completely revamped it and, after a while, the server had a concurrent player count of 20-30.

I started out as a Developer and was then promoted to Admin.

Home

Development

As a developer on SolisImperium, I setup a VPS, database and website as part of the revamp. In total, I probably contributed ~2 months of work for the server. I created some custom plugins such as a small punishment system and a staff-chat. 

Community Management

SolisImperium had a small community which I was proud of. I made various events for the players to participate in and responded to appeals and reports that the community made. I learned a lot about communities during my time at SolisImperium

New content

I worked alongside the builders and other devs to coordinate new content and planned real-time events that occurred within the server and discord. We put a lot of planning into these types of things and the end-result was met by a positive reaction.
